❓ Frequently Asked Questions

View All →
What types of diagnostic tests do you offer? ▼
We offer a comprehensive range of 500+ tests and panels including CBC, biochemistry, microbiology, serology, hormones, vitamins, thyroid, diabetes, cardiac, liver, kidney, cancer markers, and full body health packages.
How long does it take to get reports? ▼
Most routine tests like CBC, sugar, thyroid, and LFT are ready the same day (4–8 hours). Some specialized tests like culture & sensitivity take 24–72 hours. We always notify you the moment your report is ready via WhatsApp.
How do I book home sample collection? ▼
Simply call us at 07800015533, WhatsApp us, or fill the booking form on this page. Our team will confirm your slot and a trained phlebotomist will arrive at your preferred time with all sterile equipment.
Is fasting required before blood tests? ▼
Fasting (8–12 hours) is required for tests like fasting glucose, lipid profile, and liver function tests. Thyroid (TSH), vitamin tests can typically be done at any time. We advise you on preparation when you book.
Is a doctor's prescription required for testing? ▼
No, a prescription is not required for most diagnostic tests. You can walk in or book a home collection directly. However, for certain specialized tests, a referral may be helpful for proper interpretation.
How can I download or receive my reports? ▼
Your reports are sent as a PDF directly on WhatsApp and email as soon as they are ready. You can also call us on 07800015533 to check report status. No need to visit the lab again to collect physical reports.
What are your working hours? ▼
We are open 7 days a week, all year. Sample collection starts from early morning and the lab is open till 10:30 PM. Home collection can be booked from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
